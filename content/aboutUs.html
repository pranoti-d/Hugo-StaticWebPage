About Us

We at SixtyFour Data Intelligence aim to help businesses make intelligent decisions with the help of data. Using the latest technologies’ is critical but not sufficient to achieve this objective. We believe that technology should sit in the background, allowing users to just focus on what they do best – understanding the data, interpreting the data, and deriving business insights therefrom. Technology should adapt to business needs rather than the other way round. Our ability to understand data and the way users interact with data means we can build products keeping your business needs in the front.

In today’s age, the pace of technological change means being flexible and modular are the only ways to stay ahead of competition. A product that is state of the art today can get obsolete in a year or two. The only way to keep pace with technology is to build in the ability to adopt innovation quickly. This means, huge upfront investments in technology, which might get obsolete in a couple of years is a big financial and technology risk. At SixtyFour Data Intelligence our core principal is to allow our customer stay ahead of their competition by providing them the flexibility to choose, replace or blend any part of our product with their legacy infrastructure with ease and hence mitigate that risk. Our solutions are modular, flexible, and yet, highly scalable.

We operate with following core principals:

  1. Be flexible enough to accommodate change and robust enough to manage scale.
  2. Avoid locking into any particular ecosystem. Avoid complexity both in design and in execution.
  3. Focus on Business needs first and employ the best fit technology to solve the business need. Technology exists to serve the Business.
