About Us

We at SixtyFourDI aim to help our customers make business decisions at their finger tips. While modern technology is a nececessary means to achieve this goal, it is no longer sufficient to be on the cutting edge of technology. We strongly believe that user experience comes first and hence we always imagine our products first and make sure we employ the best fit tools and technolgies in shaping them.

In todays age, flexibility and modularity is the key to stay compititive because of the pace at which technologies are improving. A state of art product can get obsolete six months from today and the only way to keep pace with the innovation is to have an ability to adopt innovation quickly. Huge investments in technology that is going to be replaced soon is a high risk. At Sixtyfour DI our core principal is to allow our customer stay ahead of their competition by providing them the flexibility to choose, replace or blend any part of our product with their legacy infrastructure with ease and hence mitigate that risk.

We operate with following core principals:

  1. Be flexible enough to accommodate change and robust enough to manage scale.
  2. Innovation never costs high, monopoly does.
  3. Insights look best when they are real time.
